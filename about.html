Who We Are

The Beta Phi Chapter of TKE is a proud part of LSU's campus and TKE's global legacy. With record-breaking membership and a focus on scholarship, character, and service, we aim to build Better Men for a Better World.

What We Stand For

Leadership, Personal Growth, Lifelong Friendships, Connections, and Community Impact through philanthropy. Looking ahead we are committed to strengthening our chapter for future generations, including plans for a new chapter house to support our growing brotherhood.

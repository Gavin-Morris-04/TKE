Beta Phi Chapter's Support for St. Jude

The Beta Phi Chapter of Tau Kappa Epsilon at LSU is deeply committed to supporting St. Jude Childre's Research Hospital. Each year, we organize a variety of fundraising events—ranging from sports tournaments and benefit nights to donation drives—all aimed at supporting the hospital's groundbreaking research and patient care. Our members work tirelessly to engage the campus and local community, knowing that every contribution helps St. Jude continue its mission to treat and defeat childhood cancer and other life-threatening diseases. By coming together for this important cause, we aim to make a real difference in the lives of children and families who need it most. Stay connected with us to learn about our upcoming events and ways you can support our efforts for St. Jude. Together, we can help create a future where no child has to face cancer.
